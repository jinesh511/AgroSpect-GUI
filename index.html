Project Description

Project Objective

As Plant diseases affect the growth of their respective species along with heavy loss of food, therefore their early identification and precaution is very significant.

Motivation

Agriculture plays a critical role in providing food supply for growing population of the world. Some times, farmers can lose almost 100% of their crop due to plant diseases, this makes crop diseases a major threat to food security around the world. Diseases identification might be challenging due to the lack of the necessary data.
